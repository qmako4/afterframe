Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

AFTERFRAME offers a straightforward and fair refund policy. We want you to be happy with your subscription and will do our best to resolve any issues.

Monthly subscriptions

Our Pro subscription is billed monthly at £7.00. You may cancel at any time and will retain full Pro access until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer partial refunds for unused time within a billing period.

14-day refund guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your Pro subscription within the first 14 days of your initial purchase, you may request a full refund. To request a refund within this period, please contact us through the website with your account email address.

Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Requests made after 14 days of the initial purchase
  • Renewal charges after the initial subscription period
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service

How to cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us through the website. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period and you will not be charged again.

How to request a refund

To request a refund within the 14-day window, contact us through the website with your account email and reason for the refund request. We aim to process all refund requests within 5 business days.

Contact

For any questions about refunds or cancellations, please contact us through the website.
