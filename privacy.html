Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

AFTERFRAME is a digital subscription service providing curated AI-generated images and prompts. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following information when you use AFTERFRAME:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider, we do not store card details
  • Subscription status — whether you are a free or Pro member
  • Usage data — pages visited and features used, collected anonymously

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send you notifications about new weekly drops (only if you opt in)
  • Improve the Service based on anonymous usage data

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database provider, used to store account information
  • Our payment processor — to handle subscription billing securely

5. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the website.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely with our database provider.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the site.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the website.
